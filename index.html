Die App ist ein Lern-Timer. Damit sie funktioniert, speichert sie folgende von dir selbst eingegebene oder erzeugte Inhalte ausschließlich lokal auf deinem Gerät:

4. iCloud-Synchronisierung (optional)

Wenn du in deinem Gerät bei iCloud angemeldet bist und iCloud für die App aktiviert ist, werden die unter Punkt 3 genannten Daten über Apples Dienst CloudKit in deiner privaten iCloud gespeichert, damit sie zwischen deinen eigenen Geräten synchron bleiben.

Diese Daten liegen in deinem persönlichen iCloud-Konto. Der Entwickler hat keinen Zugriff auf diese Inhalte. Die Verarbeitung erfolgt durch Apple als Anbieter von iCloud; es gilt die Datenschutzrichtlinie von Apple. Du kannst die Synchronisierung jederzeit in den iCloud-Einstellungen deines Geräts deaktivieren.

2. Scope

This policy applies to the app “Zeitgeist” (“the App”) for iOS and macOS. It describes which data is processed when you use the App.

3. Data stored in the App

The App is a study timer. To work, it stores the following content that you enter or generate solely locally on your device:

Type of dataPurpose
Study sessions (subject, duration, date)Showing your statistics and history
Subject names and colorsOrganizing and displaying your study time
Study goals (daily/weekly)Progress display

Technically, this data is stored in a local database (SQLite) and in the app’s system settings (UserDefaults) on your device.

4. iCloud sync (optional)

If you are signed in to iCloud on your device and iCloud is enabled for the App, the data listed in section 3 is stored via Apple’s CloudKit service in your private iCloud so it stays in sync across your own devices.

This data resides in your personal iCloud account. The developer has no access to this content. Processing is carried out by Apple as the provider of iCloud; Apple’s Privacy Policy applies. You can disable sync at any time in your device’s iCloud settings.

5. Notifications / Reminders

With your permission, the App can show local reminders (notifications) on your device. These are generated and scheduled entirely locally; no data is transmitted to a server for this purpose. You can revoke notifications at any time in your device settings.

6. No data collection, no tracking, no ads

The App contains no third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising tools. No usage, location, or device data is sent to the developer or third parties. There is no tracking across apps or websites (no App Tracking / IDFA). There is no user account and no registration.

7. Legal basis

To the extent any processing under the GDPR takes place, local storage of your input is based on providing the app functionality you requested (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR) or on your consent, e.g. for notifications and iCloud sync (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR).

8. Retention

Your data is kept until you delete it in the App or remove the App from your device. Deleting the App removes the locally stored data. Data synced to iCloud can be deleted via your device’s iCloud management.

9. Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability, and objection. Because the developer does not collect or store any personal data, you can exercise these rights regarding your content directly by editing or deleting the data in the App or via iCloud. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority.

The App is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from children.
